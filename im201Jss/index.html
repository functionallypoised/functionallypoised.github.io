This is an archive of creative commons album from P-hase,

a music producer and sound designer based in Los Angeles.
Inspired by the phases of precipitation,
the archive is designed to provide an interactive experience for users to explore the rhytmic and artistic aspects of P-hase's music, via alternative engagements.

Users can interact with the playlist to select and play individual tracks,

or click master controls to mix in blended layers of the album as whole.
The page features a selector of tracks,
a media player, master controls for playback,
and a mixer for adjusting track volumes.
