P-HASE ARCHIVE

This is an archive of creative commons work by P-hase,

a music producer and sound designer based in Los Angeles.

The archive is designed to provide an interactive experience for users to explore the rhytmic and artistic aspects of P-hase's music, via alternative engagements.

The page features a custom playlist selector of tracks,
a media player, master controls for playback,
and a mixer for adjusting track volumes.

Users can interact with the playlist to select and play individual tracks,

use the master controls to manage overall playback,
and utilize the mixer to create custom mixes of the tracks.
Additionally, there is a video section that provides
a visual accompaniment to the audio content.
This page is designed to offer music in a modular, dynamic format.
